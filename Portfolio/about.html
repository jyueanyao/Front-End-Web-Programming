Fun facts about me

👩🏻‍💻 I’m a UX and product designer based in Los Angeles, pursuing a Master’s in Integrated Design, Business, and Technology at USC. Originally from Taiwan, I’m passionate about designing meaningful, user-centered digital experiences that bridge technology and human needs.

🎨 My creative roots started with fine art and visual storytelling. Over time, I discovered how design can move beyond aesthetics to solve real-world challenges—whether reimagining healthcare workflows with the American Heart Association or designing inclusive education tools for rural students.

🧠 With a foundation in Human–Computer Interaction, Visual Communication, and Computer Science, I thrive in cross-disciplinary teams that blend data, design, and technology. My projects range from building responsive web portfolios to prototyping modular footwear and exploring AI-assisted healthcare solutions.

📸 Outside of design, I express creativity through photography and video editing, capturing spontaneous city moments and subtle light. These small creative habits continuously sharpen my eye for composition, rhythm, and narrative.

🕺 I also love tap dancing, jogging, and mindfulness practice—each helping me find balance, energy, and clarity to design with empathy and joy.
